Lima

Lima is the capital and largest city of Peru. It is located on the central coast of the country, on the shores of the Pacific Ocean. It is the political, economic, cultural, and commercial center of the country. Lima is known for its architecture, its museums, and its renowned cuisine, considered one of the best in the world.
